Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22/09/2024

Thank you for using Manga Extension. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. No Data Collection

Manga Extension does not collect, store, or share any personal, sensitive, or usage data. All information related to your preferences and usage is stored locally on your own device and is not transmitted outside of your browser.

2. Local Storage Only

The extension exclusively uses the Chrome browser's local storage to save your settings, preferences, and any manga data you choose to manage within the extension. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers or third parties.

3. No External Requests or Advertisements

Manga Extension does not make any external requests, nor does it include third-party services such as analytics, tracking, or advertising networks. You can use the extension without concerns about your data being exposed to any outside sources.

4. No Access to Sensitive Information

We do not access, request, or store any personal or sensitive information such as passwords, browsing history, or payment details.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, feel free to contact us:
